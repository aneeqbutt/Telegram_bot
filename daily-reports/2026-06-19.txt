CHALLENGES RESOLVED

Articles stored as Uncategorized not being posted to Telegram

* Root cause:
  Articles were scraped before init_db.py was run so the keywords collection was empty.
  classify_article() returned Uncategorized for every article. Those articles are stored
  permanently with that category and the dispatcher filters them out intentionally.

* Fix:
  Created reclassify.py to do a one-time update — loads keywords into memory, fetches all
  Uncategorized unposted articles, re-classifies each one, and updates the category in MongoDB.
  Future articles are unaffected as they are classified correctly at insert time.
